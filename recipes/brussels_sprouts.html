Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Balsamic and Honey

Roasted brussels sprouts with balsamic and honey

Prep: 10 mins

Cook: 30 mins

Total: 40 mins

Servings: 4

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking Steps:

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper

Step 2: Combine Brussels sprouts, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; toss to coat. Spread out evenly on the prepared baking sheet.

Step 3: Roast in the preheated oven until browned, about 30 minutes, turning after 15 minutes. Remove from oven and drizzle with balsamic vinegar and honey, mixing to coat evenly.

Ingredients:

  1. 1lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed
  2. 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  3. 1 teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste
  4. 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or to taste
  5. 1 tablespoon good quality balsamic vinegar
  6. 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste

Nutrition Facts:

Per Serving: 175 calories; protein 3.9g; carbohydrates 19.6g; fat 10.5g; sodium 509.9mg.
