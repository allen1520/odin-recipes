Macaroni and Cheese

Southern style macaroni and cheese

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 40 mins

Total: 55 mins

Servings: 8

Yield: 8 servings

Cooking Steps:

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).

Step 2: Spray a 9" x 13" casserole dish with cooking spray

Step 3: Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil. Stir in the macaroni and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until it has cooked through but still slightly firm (~8 minutes); drain.

Step 4: Slowly melt butter in a large pot over low heat. Stir in evaporated milk and black pepper; cook until heated through.

Step 5: Whisk eggs in a bowl until smooth; whisk about 1/2 cup of evaporated milk mixture into the eggs, 1 tablespoon at a time, until thoroughly incorporated. Whiak the egg mixture ack into the saucepan with evaporated milk mixture. Continue to heat gently, whisking constantly, until the evaporated milk mixture almost comes to a simmer and the sauce thickens.

Step 6: Gently mix salt and cooked macaroni into the sauce and stir to combine.

Step 7: Spoon 1/4 of the macaroni mixture in a layer into the bottom of the prepared casserole dish and top with 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese. Repeat layers three more times; top last cheese layer with paprika to taste.

Step 8: Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is bubbling, about 20 minutes.

Ingredients:

  1. Cooking spray
  2. 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  3. 1/2 cup butter
  4. 2 (12oz) cans evaporated milk
  5. 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  6. 3 eggs
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  9. 1 pinch paprika, or to taste (Optional)
